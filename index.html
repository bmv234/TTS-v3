About CSM Text-to-Speech

This application uses the Conversational Speech Model (CSM) to generate natural-sounding speech from text.

CSM is designed to maintain speaker identity and conversational context, providing high-quality speech synthesis.

Different speaker IDs can be selected to generate speech with different voice characteristics.

Note: Speech generation may take 1-2 minutes on CPU. Please be patient during processing.
